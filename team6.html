Our Team

Rajat Khanna

Senior Advisor

Rajat is the Chartered Investment Manager and CEO of SSL Wealth Management, a private bank which advises clients across the globe in structuring diversified portfolio investments in Gold, Real Estate, Private Debt, Equity and Hedge Funds. Rajat started his career at Goldman Sachs where he oversaw over $8 billion of M&A transactions, he was particularly involved in the merger of Prudential with Wachovia, which created the 3rd largest brokerage with over $500 billion in assets. He then went on manage a $150 million long/short equity portfolio with a $1.5 billion AuM hedge fund at Libra Advisors. Rajat then joined Credit Suisse where he traded convertible bonds while overseeing over $200 million in principal assets, and further went on to trade over $10 billion of bonds across the capital structure with a focus on North American markets.

Rajat holds a Bs in Applied Economics and Management from Cornell University where he graduated with distinction, and holds an MBA in Finance and Entrepreneurship from Insead. Rajat also holds a finance certificate from McGill University.
