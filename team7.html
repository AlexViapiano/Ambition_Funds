Sandy Marks

Senior Advisor

Sandy Marks is a Principal and Co-Founder of Clearnote Capital. Since its inception in 2014, Clearnote Capital has financed dozens of real-estate transactions in NYC and nationwide, including acquiring buildings in Astoria, Queens and completing the ground-up construction of a 27,000 square foot residential apartment in New Yorkâ€™s Little Italy. In addition, Mr. Marks is the Co-Founder of Clear Energy Capital, a New York-based company financing the development of solar grids throughout the United States. Current projects for Clear Energy Capital are comprised of several developments in New Mexico and Highland, NY.

Prior to founding these companies, Mr. Marks was a Senior Management Advisor at JP Morgan Chase, where he financed the largest transaction in CTL history: a $225,000,000 loan on 37 buildings in San Francisco, CA. Furthermore, Mr. Marks has also worked as a Financial Analyst for Sterling Equities and has developed numerous other properties, such as the 75,000 square foot McBirney YMCA Condominium conversion in NYC.

Mr. Marks holds a BS in Finance from the University of Maryland Smith School of Business.
