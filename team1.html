Our Team

Yassin El Naggar

CEO

Yassin El Naggar is the Founder and CEO of Ambition Funds & Ambition Management LLC. Yassin started his career while still studying at university as an independent financial advisor, he managed independent portfolios for private investors. Yassin ended up managing a majority of the equity portfolio of his clients and that is when the idea of Ambition Funds came into place. Yassin partnered up with the most reputable firms in the world to create Ambition Fund I and continue the business of Ambition Management LLC as an SEC Registered Investment Advisor.

Yassin currently serves on the Board of Directors of Massawa International Petroleum (Upstream oil and gas exploration company), Great Foods (Food Production Company), and Mentin LTD (Real Estate Development Firm in the UK).

Yassin holds a BComm in Finance from John Molson School of Business where he graduated with distinction.
