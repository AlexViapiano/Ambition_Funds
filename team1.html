Our Team

Yassin El Naggar

CEO

Yassin El Naggar is the CEO and Founder of Ambition Funds, where he focuses on investing the portfolio. He was previously managing multiple independent portfolios for individual investors. Yassin started his career joining his family business and in the span of two years from $0 in sales became one of the largest canned goods importers in Canada and USA. He then quit a very lucrative business to pursue his passion of investing.

Yassin currently sits on the Board of Directors of Massawa Petroleum International, Alex Scan, Great Foods, and Mentin LTD (UK).

Yassin holds a BComm in Finance from John Molson School of Business where he graduated with distinction.
