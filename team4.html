Our Team

Kosta Velis

Senior Advisor

Kosta Velis is a twenty-four year veteran of the U.S. and International High Net Worth Investment Advisory industry. Having started his career as a successful Financial Advisor with Merrill Lynch, he later joined Smith Barney, Citigroup for their Investment Management Consulting Group. During his tenure with Smith Barney, Kosta was promoted throughout his career to managing one of their top decile Complexes in New Orleans. Along the way, he distinguished himself in the management ranks by earning his Certified Investment Management Analyst (CIMA) and Certified Financial Planner (CFP) designations while focusing on growing his knowledge of Alternative Investments and Asset Allocation. Kosta comes to Ambition Funds after management in some high profile international multi-family offices, further enhancing his knowledge of offshore funds, alternative investments, and the Ultra-High-Net-Worth industry.

Kosta is a graduate of Southern Methodist University with a B.A. in Political Science. He is also a graduate of the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania's Investment Management Consulting program where is received his Certified Investment Management Analyst (CIMA) designation.
