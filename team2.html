Our Team

Dr. Kosrow Denhad

Senior Advisor

Dr. Dehnad is an adjunct professor at Columbia University and is Chief Data Scientist and Director of Analytics at Aims Consulting. Prior to this position, he was Director of Analytics and Chief Data Scientist at Concord Advice.

Previously, Dr. Dehnad was Assistant General Manger and head of Analytic and Quantitative Trading and CoHead of Hedge Fund Group at Samba Financial Group where he initiated use of Big Data and Data Mining to develop trading strategies. Before joining Samba, he was a Managing Director in the Structured Credit Products at CITIGROUP in charge of Exotic Credit Trading. Previously, he was head of Fixed Income Derivatives Structuring and New Products. Prior to the merger of CITIBANK and Travelers group, he was the head of Hybrid Desk at CITIBANK that he joined from Chase Manhattan Bank where he was head of New Derivatives Products and Structuring and created products such as Flexible Cap, Q-Cap, and Defensive Swap etc.

Academically, Dr. Dehnad is also Adjunct Professor of Finance and Risk Engineering at New York University and has also taught at San Jose State University and Rutgers University. He has publishes a number of articles is areas of finance, computer security, and quality control.

Dr. Dehnad received his BSc. in Mathematics with first class honors from University of Manchester in England and his PhD. in Mathematics from University of California, Berkeley. After receiving his second Doctorate from Stanford University, he joined AT&T Bell Labs where he published the book “Quality Control and Taguchi Method”.
